I am currently an associate professor and a doctoral advisor in School of Computer Science and Technology, Harbin Institute of Technology (Shenzhen). I received my Ph.D. degree from School of Software, Tsinghua University, advised by Mingsheng Long. I received my B.S. degree from Department of Electrical Engineering, Tsinghua University.

My research spans machine learning algorithms and applications. I am working on data-efficient learning, which includes transfer learning, semi-supervised learning and multi-task learning. I am also interested in related topics in machine learning and deep learning, such as foundation models, time series analysis and multi-modal learning.
